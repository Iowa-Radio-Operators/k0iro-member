Welcome to the K0IRO Membership Management Center

We’re excited that you’re considering joining a statewide community built for radio enthusiasts of all backgrounds. K0IRO exists to provide a welcoming space for operators to connect, especially in areas where a local club isn’t available or where gathering opportunities are limited. Our goal is simple: create a supportive, accessible, and engaging environment for amateur radio operators across Iowa.

Before applying, we encourage you to explore a few key pages that explain who we are and what we stand for:

  • About Us
  • Mission & Vision
  • Club Charter & Bylaws

These documents outline our purpose, values, and the framework that guides our club.

You’ll also find information about membership dues. At this time, we are not yet set up to accept dues payments — and that’s perfectly fine. As a new organization, our officers are finalizing the administrative details behind the scenes. When everything is ready, we’ll reach out to early members to complete the dues process.

If everything sounds good, simply click Apply in the upper‑right corner, fill out the form, and you’ll be on your way. We’re glad you’re here and look forward to welcoming you into the K0IRO community.

If you have any questions or run into issues during sign‑up, please email info@k0iro.com and someone will be happy to assist.
